We’re sure you’ll agree that there’s nothing like being surrounded by the beautiful colour and fragrance of sumptuous flowers during the summer months. Florals are an integral part of interior and fashion trends and we are thrilled that summer is nearly here - it’s a great excuse to fill your home with fresh, beautiful blooms and welcome the warmth of a summer garden into your interior styling.

Inspired by British country gardens, Waterperry fabrics and wallpapers by Sanderson, embodies a relaxed, painterly style and celebrates the horticultural finesse found throughout English gardens and countryside. Renowned for hand drawn and painted designs, Sanderson brings a fresh twist to the classic floral print with an effortless, relaxed elegance.
