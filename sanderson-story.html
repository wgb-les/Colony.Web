The History

It all started in 1900 with a small collection of paints designed to complement the Arthur Sanderson & Sons wallpaper collections.

Today, you will find yourself spoilt for choice with shades of Sanderson paint - a comprehensive range of paint featuring 140 shades. The timelessly elegant palette has been designed with both traditional and contemporary homes in mind and specifically curated to complement our fabric and wallpaper collections.

The Finishes

Sanderson paints are bursting with the best grade pigments to ensure quality of colour, excellent coverage and a smooth application that professionals love. Every shade is available in four finishes, giving you the opportunity to work across your entire home. From our elegant, chalky Matt Emulsion to our durable and versatile eggshell, you will find the perfect finish for walls, woodwork and metalwork. Even the most lively of family homes can enjoy the Sanderson touch with our Active Emulsion - specifically designed for hard-working spaces such as hallways, family rooms and kitchens.

Made in the UK

All Sanderson paints are made in the UK and you’ll be glad to know that we’ve formulated our paints to minimise VOCs (volatile organic compounds, which contribute to atmospheric pollution). Our water-based paints contain virtually no VOCs and our oil-based paints are as low as possible.
