The Zoffany and Sanderson paint collections offer a wide range of luxuriously elegant shades, all available in multiple finishes allowing you to work across your entire home. For best results, always use a paint designed for the surface you are decorating.

Zoffany paint finishes

Zoffany flat emulsion

This water-based emulsion dries to an exceptionally chalky flat finish that softly absorbs light and emphasises the deep pigments in each colour. With a gently spongeable surface, our Flat Emulsion delivers undeniable sophistication for interior walls and ceilings. It can also be used in bathrooms and kitchens, but only if they are very well ventilated and the painted wall does not come into direct contact with water.

  • Dries in 1-2 hours, recoatable after 4 hours.
  • Apply by brush or roller
  • Zoffany Flat Emulsion water-based paint contains only 2g/l VOC the EU limit for this product (Cat A/a WB) is 30g/l (2010).

Zoffany elite emulsion

Our elite emulsion is a washable and stain resistant paint for modern living. This water-based finish is ideal for kitchens, children's rooms and high-traffic areas. Following extensive research, and by listening to our decorating partners, this dynamic paint has been specifically developed for maximum performance, whilst achieving the lowest sheen possible.

  • Dries in 1-2 hours, recoatable after 4 hours.
  • Apply by brush or roller.
  • Zoffany Elite Emulsion water-based paint contains only 2g/l VOC the EU limit for this product (Cat A/a WB) is 30g/l (2010).

Zoffany acrylic eggshell

This hard-wearing and washable water-based paint is suitable for interior walls, ceilings, woodwork, radiators and metalwork. Acrylic Eggshell provides a low sheen finish that makes it perfect for kitchens, bathrooms and conservatories. It is also suitable for exterior use on woodwork and metals (with a suitable primer), such as front doors, window frames and townhouse railings.

  • Dries in 1-2 hours to a washable finish resistant to condensation, recoatable after 4 hours.
  • Apply by brush or roller.
  • Zoffany Acrylic Eggshell water-based paint contains only 2g/l VOC the EU limit for this product (Cat A/a WB) is 30g/l (2010).

Zoffany eggshell

This traditional oil-based paint gives a low sheen finish that is resistant to steam and condensation in kitchens, bathrooms and conservatories. Its inherent durability makes Zoffany Eggshell ideal for interior woodwork and metalwork including doors, window frames, skirting boards and radiators. It is also suitable for use on exterior woodwork and metals (with a suitable primer).

  • Dries in 2-3 hours, recoatable overnight, apply by brush or roller.
  • Zoffany Eggshell oil based paint contains only 297g/l VOC the EU limit for this product (Cad A/d SB) is 300g/l (2010).

Sanderson paint finishes

Sanderson matt emulsion

For that classic flat chalky finish, Sanderson Matt Emulsion brings exceptional coverage and an undeniable elegance to interior walls and ceilings. It leaves a gently spongeable surface that is ideal for most rooms with a small word of caution for bathrooms and kitchens; these rooms must very well ventilated and the painted wall should not come into direct contact with water.

Sanderson active emulsion

This high-performance product is ideal for busy family homes where paintwork can be subjected to tough love. Ideal for corridors, kitchens, and playrooms, Sanderson Active Emulsion is highly durable and can be used on walls and woodwork. It looks great too - with only a 6% sheen level, it achieves that classic matt appearance whilst maintaining a smooth, washable finish.

Sanderson water-based eggshell

Sanderson Acrylic Eggshell is a hard-wearing, water-based paint suitable for interior walls, ceilings, woodwork, radiators and metalwork. It has a washable, low sheen finish, making it perfect for kitchens, bathrooms and conservatories. Also suitable for use on interior and exterior woodwork and metals which have been primed.

Sanderson oil-based eggshell

This traditional oil-based paint gives a low sheen finish that is resistant to steam and condensation, making it ideal for kitchens, bathrooms and conservatories. It’s also durable enough for interior and exterior woodwork and metalwork (with a suitable primer) including doors, window frames, skirting boards and radiators.

Aftercare advice

General care

  • Avoid grease from building up by keeping rooms well ventilated while you are cooking.
  • Protect painted surfaces during any home improvements.
  • For general dusting use a paper towel or soft cloth and the damp dusting method.
  • To prevent marking, always use the soft brush attachment with the vacuum cleaner
  • Avoid placing heavy objects on newly painted woodwork and metalwork for at least a week.

Cleaning

Use a diluted lukewarm solution of domestic washing up liquid and a clean sponge or lint-free cloth. Ensure your sponge or cloth is wet but not dripping.

Dab the affected area and repeat as required. Be particularly gentle with emulsion-painted surfaces and avoid rubbing or circular motions as this may polish up the surface. For textured walls, a cloth should be used rather than a sponge.

Ensure radiators are at room temperature before attempting to clean them. Cleaning a hot radiator may result in localised or uneven polishing of the surface.

Repair

Lightly rub down the damaged area and apply a suitable primer to any exposed wood or metal. Repaint the damaged area with one or two coats as required. Stand-alone items (such as window frames and doors) may need completely repainting to avoid seeing any ‘joins’ between the existing finish and the repair.
